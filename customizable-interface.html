Customizable Interface

The settings interface is designed for straightforward customization, empowering you to tailor the appâ€™s appearance and functionality to your personal study habits and preferences. A subtle, grey-toned animated gear in the background reinforces the idea of fine-tuning your learning environment.

Customization Options:

  • Background Style: Choose from themes like solid color, frosted glass, or dark mode to create a visual environment that is both calming and focused.
  • Text Color Selection: Customize text colors throughout the app to improve contrast and readability, ensuring that your study materials are easy on the eyes.
  • Exam Countdown Visibility & Input: Toggle the display of an exam countdown on the home page and enter your exam date to receive a gentle reminder of the time remaining to prepare.
  • Username Input: Personalize your experience by entering your name so that the app can address you directly within content modules and quizzes.
  • Avatar Selection: Choose from a variety of teacher avatars that present quiz questions and offer guidance, creating a more engaging and supportive learning experience.
  • Text-to-Speech (TTS) Configuration: Quickly access your device's accessibility settings to select a preferred system voice for reading content aloud.
  • Enable Extra Time: Activate extra time accommodations for quizzes and timed activities, ensuring you have the flexibility needed to fully absorb the material.

By providing these extensive customization options, ThisalLearn ensures that every student can create a study environment that feels personal, comfortable, and optimally supportive of their learning journey.
