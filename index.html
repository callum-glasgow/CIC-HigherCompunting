Leveraging Swift and SwiftUI for optimal performance and user experience.

With widespread use of iPads in Scottish schools, developing a native iPad app using Swift and SwiftUI was the ideal choice. Here's why:

  • Seamless Integration: Tight integration with Apple’s ecosystem using CloudKit, Push Notifications, and Widgets.
  • Resource Efficiency: Optimized for performance and battery life, even on older devices.
  • Accessibility: Built-in support for VoiceOver, Dynamic Type, and Switch Control.
  • Rapid Development: SwiftUI’s declarative syntax speeds up development and iteration.

Choosing Swift ensures ThisalLearn is optimized for your devices, offering a smooth and engaging experience.

About ThisalLearn

Innovating education through technology.

ThisalLearn is designed specifically for Scottish high school students studying SQA Higher Computing. Our app provides an accessible and engaging platform for revision and learning.

Inspired by Apple’s minimalist design, we strive to offer an intuitive and modern experience that bridges the gap between available resources and student needs.

Modular Design

Flexible, updatable, and scalable content architecture.

ThisalLearn utilizes a modular architecture where all text content and quiz questions are stored in structured JSON files. This approach offers several benefits:

  • Easy Updates: Content can be quickly updated or corrected without needing to redistribute the entire app.
  • Expandability: New topics or course materials can be added seamlessly, scaling to include different levels like National 5 or Advanced Higher.
  • Version Control: Using CloudKit for synchronization ensures that students always have access to the most current materials.
  • Offline Access: JSON files are loaded into memory at startup, allowing for fast, responsive navigation even without internet connectivity.

This modular design ensures that ThisalLearn remains a dynamic and up-to-date resource, adapting to curriculum changes and student needs with ease.
