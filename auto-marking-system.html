Auto-Marking System

Our application incorporates an AI-based auto-marking system designed to evaluate open-ended exam-style questions. This system leverages advanced models from OpenAI—most notably, ChatGPT-4 Turbo—to provide accurate, immediate, and personalized feedback on your responses.

What is OpenAI? OpenAI is an AI research lab that develops cutting-edge language models capable of understanding and generating human-like text. Their API enables our app to harness these capabilities for tasks such as content generation and automated grading. OpenAI is committed to strong privacy standards; you can review their API Privacy Policy for more information.

How the Auto-Marking System Works:

  • Exam Question & Answer Submission: You are presented with a specific past paper question and a text box to enter your answer.
  • Content Moderation: Upon submission, OpenAI's Content Moderation API checks your response to ensure it meets appropriate standards.
  • Marking Process: Once your answer passes moderation, your response—along with the exam question, maximum mark, and detailed marking instructions (including sample answers and additional context)—is compiled into a system message.
  • Automated Evaluation: This system message instructs ChatGPT-4 Turbo to act as an automarker. It then evaluates your answer and responds with a mark out of the maximum possible, along with a detailed explanation.
  • Verification: You can review the marking scheme to verify the accuracy of the model's assessment.

Why ChatGPT-4 Turbo? ChatGPT-4 Turbo is our model of choice for several compelling reasons:

Compared to previous models like GPT-3.5 Turbo, ChatGPT-4 Turbo delivers superior natural language understanding and nuanced reasoning. While the standard GPT-4 model is highly capable, ChatGPT-4 Turbo is optimized for cost-effectiveness and rapid token processing. Its high token speed ensures that you receive feedback almost instantaneously—an essential feature for a real-time, responsive assessment system.

Additionally, ChatGPT-4 Turbo balances accuracy with efficiency. It is less expensive to operate than the full GPT-4 model, making it ideal for continuous, high-volume use in an educational setting. Its inaxspecaty—its capability to understand and process complex queries swiftly—ensures that every response is delivered promptly, without compromising on detail or quality.

In summary, ChatGPT-4 Turbo’s advanced language comprehension, rapid processing speed, and cost-effectiveness make it the best choice for our auto-marking system, delivering fast, reliable, and insightful evaluations of your exam responses.
