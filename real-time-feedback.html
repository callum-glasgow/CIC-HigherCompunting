Real-Time Feedback

Quizzes present a series of multiple-choice questions hosted by the chosen avatar. Students receive immediate feedback on whether they answered correctly, followed by an animated transition to the next question.

After completing a quiz, the app visualizes your performance with a dynamic pie chart that details the percentage of correct answers. This visual summary is complemented by a set of color-coded mastery indicators: green signifies all questions answered correctly, yellow indicates that at least one answer was incorrect, and red shows that all responses were incorrect.

The familiar multiple-choice format, reminiscent of popular platforms like Kahoot, makes the quizzes both interactive and engaging. The real-time feedback is designed not only to reinforce learning immediately but also to help you identify areas that may require additional practice.
